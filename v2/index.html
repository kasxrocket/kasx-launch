How to Mint KasX

Mobile (Telegram)

  1. Open Telegram app.
  2. Find and chat with KSPR bot.
  3. Send the command to mint KasX.
  4. Follow the bot's instructions.

Desktop (Chrome, Kasware Wallet)

  1. Open Chrome browser.
  2. Access your Kasware Wallet.
  3. Copy your private key securely.
  4. Open Telegram and chat with KSPR bot.
  5. Paste your private key and follow bot's instructions.
